WHY MOLDOVA?

Born With Wine

The little known European country of Moldova is putting its name on the map for its fantastic wines. The rich flavor of these wines is fast becoming recognised by world-renowned wine experts. Moldovan wine has great texture, warmth and flavour that will surprise and delight. “Moldovans consider wine to be in the blood of their people. They are born with wine, live with wine and die with wine.”

Geography

Moldova has 112 thousand hectares of vineyard planted with over 50 types of technical varieties.

Climate

The temperate climate which is moderately-continental has allowed the locals to produce and perfect their wines.

Soil

Rich black soil overlying limestone bedrock full of fossilised shells, perfect for vineyards.

Grapes

Unique indigenous grape varieties: Feteasca Alba, Feteasca Neagra, Rara Neagra, among others.

Taste

Authentic texture, warmth and flavour.

Winemaking

Produced with care by talented winemakers using local grapes as well as international ones, adjusted to the local terroir.
