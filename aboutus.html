About us

ABOUT US

We're the connection

We. Love. Wine. Not only do we love wine, but we want to bring excellent wines to our customers. This is why we focus exclusively on Moldovan wines. Although Moldova is a little-known wine region, it can boast some of the richest wine culture in the world.

THE BEGINNINGS

Our Origin

Based in Copenhagen, Denmark, MoldoVin has been providing the Danish wine market with high quality Moldovan wine since 2017.

It all started after a trip to Moldova. We were so fascinated by the quality of their homemade wines and all the traditions around it, that we felt we needed to share that well kept secret with other wine enthusiasts in Scandinavia.
